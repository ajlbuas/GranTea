Our company, called GranTea, is dedicated to bringing people together and fostering meaningful connections through our events. We believe that community is essential for personal growth and well-being, and that authentic connections are the foundation of strong communities. We infuse positivity and sincerity into every aspect of our events, from the planning stages to the execution. We strive to create an environment where attendees feel welcomed, supported, and encouraged to be their true selves.

Community stands for being able to completely be yourself in a group of people. It stands for being able to share fun experiences. Community is having a social circle of friends. 

Sincerity is the virtue of one who communicates and acts in accordance with the entirety of their feelings, beliefs, thoughts, and desires in a manner that is honest and genuine. We believe that this is something very important when it comes to connecting with one another. Honesty and being genuine will bring us together!

Connection is one of our core principles. We believe that regardless of age, human being belong with others. We should not let others fall behind and so we made it our duty to (re)connect the elderly with others to respark the joy of life. What would life be without our loved ones

One of our main values we stand for is positivity. Life is about happiness. Many elderly people forget and try to ignore the fact that life doesn’t stop when they get old and retire. It really depends on the specific person how positive he/she is about the things happening around. With our brand we want to remind elderly people that there are dozens of reasons to spend the day with a smile on their face. Bringing out the positive side of being a pensioner to the front for our audience is one of our main priorities.
