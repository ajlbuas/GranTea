Our activities

We infuse positivity and sincerity into every aspect of our events, from the planning stages to the execution. We strive to create an environment where attendees feel welcomed, supported, and encouraged to be their true selves.

That is why we created different yet all very sociable events for the elderly to join. Whether it's something laidback or active, there is something for everyone.

To introduce our activities, see the following.

Zumba

Get ready to shake, shimmy, and sweat your way to a healthier you with Zoemba! Our dance fitness class combines the best of Zumba and Brazilian rhythms to create an exhilarating workout that's as fun as it is effective.

If you're looking for a fun and exciting way to stay active, Zoemba is the perfect choice. Our experienced instructors will guide you through a series of easy-to-follow dance moves that will get your heart pumping and your body moving. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or a complete beginner, Zoemba is a great way to get fit and have fun at the same time.

Golfing

Always wanted to try golfing? This is your chance!

Our golf class is an easy and fun way to stay active while making new friends. It is the perfect way to enjoy an afternoon in the sun!

Our experienced golf instructors will take you by the hand in learning everything you need to know about golf. No need to be an experienced golfer since this class is meant to learn new things. Golf is a great way to stay active and work towards a healthy lifestyle. Plus, the best thing is, you can do it all while making new friends!

If we got you interested, sign up for our golf class today! Start your journey towards new experiences and making new friends. Sign-up is open now, so don’t wait any longer and meet us at the golf court!

Chess

Chess is one of the oldest games in the world. Many great leaders have implemented the startegy and tactics from the board game.

Well, let’s learn from the history of this beautiful and focus on the pleasant moments this sport can give you. We can assure you that on our organised chess gatherings you will feel only positive and pleasent emotions. Not only that! Chess helps you improve memory function and increases creativity in the individuals! On top of all these are just few of the benefits the biggest game of minds will give you.

Be part of this amazing community and reserve your spot for the chess night events we have prepared for you!

Guarantee your membership and we will guarantee you amazing memories!
